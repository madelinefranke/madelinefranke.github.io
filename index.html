BIO


Madeline Franke is originally from Annapolis, Maryland, but has resided in Powder Springs, Georgia for the past ten years with her family and two dogs. She graduated with honors from Harrison High School in 2019.

Madeline is a 2nd year psychology major at the University of Georgia. Athens has become her home away from home.After earning her B.S. in Psychology, Madeline plans on going to graduate school in order to pursue a career in counseling. In addition, she plans on being a creative writer and becoming a published author.

WORK EXPERIENCE


For the past year, Madeline has held a position as a Resident Assistant at Brumby Hall, a freshman residence hall. As an RA, she lives on a hallway with thirty freshman students with the responsibility of developing community, ensuring safety, enforcing policy, and providing a safe and comfortable environment for residents. With the pandemic, the position has become more challenging, as RAs are expected to enforce mask and visitation policies. However, Madeline has found this position to be rewarding as she has been able to form new connections, user her skills to help others, expand her work experience, and grow as a person. She plans on being an RA again for the 2021-2022 academic year.
